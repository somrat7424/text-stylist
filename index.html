FAQ

What is a Stylish Name Generator? ▾

A stylish name generator converts your regular text into fancy Unicode characters that look like different fonts. They can be copied and pasted anywhere that supports text input.

How does fancy text work? ▾

Fancy text uses Unicode — a universal character standard — that appear as stylish letters in any app, without needing a special font installed.

Yes! Most styles work in Free Fire and PUBG Mobile. Copy the name and paste it into the nickname field in your game settings.
